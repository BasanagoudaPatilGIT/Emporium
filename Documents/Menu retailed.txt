2. Features

2. Owner
a. Define Employees.
b. Add/Edit Product master.
c. View/Approve Orders.
d. Order to Bill.
e. Bill.
f. Stock Browser.
g. Reports.
h. Stock Movement(maintain transfer type - Online-Offline/Offline-Online).

3. Employee
a. Order to Bill.
b. Bill.
c. Stock Browser.
